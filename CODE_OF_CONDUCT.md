# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, practical, and welcoming project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Respect different backgrounds, languages, and experience levels.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.

Reports can be raised through the repository maintainers. Maintainers should handle reports fairly and privately where possible.
